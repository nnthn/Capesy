Who we are

Capesy Natural Extractors is a farm to extract essential oil manufacturer based in Kerala, India. We grow, harvest, and steam distill over 35 natural oils and oleoresins from our own micro farming plantations delivering consistent quality to food, fragrance, pharma, and wellness industries worldwide.
